Frequently Asked Questions

When is Abhigyan '25?

Abhigyan '25 is scheduled for March 15-20, 2025.

How do I register for events?

You can register for individual events through our Events page or contact our registration team directly.

Is there a registration fee?

Registration fees vary by event. Please check individual event details or contact us for specific information.

Can students from other colleges participate?

Yes! Abhigyan '25 welcomes participants from all colleges and universities.

Are there accommodation facilities?

We can help arrange accommodation for outstation participants. Please contact us in advance.

How can my company sponsor Abhigyan '25?

We offer various sponsorship packages. Please contact our sponsorship team for detailed information.
