About Abhigyan 25

Abhigyan 25 represents the pinnacle of sustainable innovation and technological excellence. Our theme "Sustainable Synergy" embodies the perfect harmony between cutting-edge technology and environmental responsibility.

ECE Department SRMIST VDP Campus presents this grand symposium showcasing the future of green technology, sustainable engineering practices, and innovative solutions for global challenges.

Innovation is Calling!

The ECE Department, SRMIST VDP Campus, proudly presents Abhigyan'25 - a spectacular tech fest. With the theme "Sustainable Synergy," this edition is all about uniting technology with sustainability to shape a better tomorrow. From thrilling technical events that test your skills to exciting non-technical events packed with creativity and fun, Abhigyan'25 offers something for every innovator and dreamer.

Get ready for a journey where knowledge meets collaboration, and every idea has the power to inspire change.
