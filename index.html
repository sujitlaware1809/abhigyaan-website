About Abhigyan 25

Abhigyan 25 represents the pinnacle of sustainable innovation and technological excellence. Our theme "Sustainable Synergy" embodies the perfect harmony between cutting-edge technology and environmental responsibility.

SRMIST Vadapalani's Department of Electronics & Communication Engineering presents this grand symposium showcasing the future of green technology, sustainable engineering practices, and innovative solutions for global challenges.

🌱

Sustainability Focus

Every event and competition is designed with environmental consciousness, promoting green technology and sustainable engineering practices.

🚀

Innovation Hub

Cutting-edge research presentations, breakthrough technology showcases, and revolutionary ideas that shape the future of engineering.

🤝

Collaborative Learning

Fostering collaboration between students, researchers, and industry experts to create synergistic solutions for global challenges.

🎯

Future Ready

Preparing the next generation of engineers with skills and knowledge essential for sustainable technological advancement.

Our Mission

To create a platform where sustainable technology meets innovative engineering, fostering the development of eco-friendly solutions that address real-world challenges while inspiring the next generation of environmentally conscious engineers and technologists.

Past Editions

2024

Abhigyan 2024: "Bendable Innovation"

A groundbreaking symposium focused on flexible electronics and adaptive technologies. Featured over 500 participants, 15 competitions, and showcased revolutionary developments in bendable displays, flexible solar panels, and wearable technology. The event established new standards for innovation in adaptive electronics.

2023

Abhigyan 2023: "AI Renaissance"

The renaissance of artificial intelligence took center stage with cutting-edge AI demonstrations, machine learning competitions, and deep learning workshops. Over 800 participants explored the transformative power of AI in various domains including healthcare, environmental monitoring, and smart infrastructure.

2022

Abhigyan 2022: "Digital Transformation"

Embracing the digital revolution with focus on IoT, cloud computing, and digital twin technologies. The event featured industry partnerships with leading tech companies and provided hands-on experience with emerging digital technologies that are reshaping industries.

2021

Abhigyan 2021: "Connected Future"

A virtual symposium that brought together minds from across the globe during challenging times. Focused on 5G technologies, edge computing, and the future of connectivity. Despite being online, it achieved record participation with attendees from over 20 countries.

2020

Abhigyan 2020: "Smart Systems"

The inaugural edition that laid the foundation for all future events. Focused on smart city technologies, automation, and intelligent systems. Though smaller in scale, it established the tradition of excellence and innovation that continues to define Abhigyan.
