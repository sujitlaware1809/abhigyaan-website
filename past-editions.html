Abhigyan 2024

"Bendable Innovation"

Abhigyan 2024 focused on flexible electronics and adaptive technologies. The symposium featured groundbreaking presentations on flexible displays, wearable electronics, and bendable circuit designs. Over 500 participants from 50+ colleges showcased their innovative projects.
